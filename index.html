Cyber Security  


TYPES OF CYBERSECURITY

Network Security – protects a computer network from intruders by targetting variety of threats and stops them from entering or spreading on the network.

Information security – protects the data/information from unauthorized access to avoid identity theft and to protect privacy and integrity.

Application security – aims at keeping software and devices free of threats.These measures are taken during the development cycle or design stage to protect applications from threats that can come through flaws in the application design or maintenance.

Disaster recovery – defines how an organization/business responds to an incident of cyber-attack or any other event that causes the loss of operations or data.This process includes developing recovery strategies in case of a disaster.Thus any business must have a solid plan for disaster recovery to resume normal business operations as quickly as possible after a disaster.


TYPES OF CYBERTHREATS

Malware – Also known as Malicious software which is designed intentionally by cyber-attackers to cause damage to a user's computer or server. There are different types of malwares such as computer viruses, spyware, Trojans, Ransomware, botnets and keyloggers.

Ransomware – It is a type of malware that locks down a user's data or encrypts it until a ransom is paid.

Phishing Attacks – When cyber attackers use emails which appear to be from a legitimate company or a trusted source to target their victims asking for sensitive information such as credit card details and other personal information and duping them. It can also be through phone calls or text-messages.

Social engineering – It is the psychological manipulation of user's to obtain confidential information or into performing some action which may not be in their best interests.
